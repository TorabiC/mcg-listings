Property overview

Nineteen estates,
one gated address.

The Enclave is a private, gated community of nineteen estate homesites in the wooded hills off North Park Road, just above Beaver Lake — the 28,000-acre Ozark reservoir that forms the headwaters of the White River.

Lots range from one to more than five acres, threaded by quiet private drives around central community spaces, moments from the Prairie Creek Marina on the lake. The property is offered as an entire development, with individual estate lots available.
