Amenities & lake life

A rare address, minutes from the region's center.

On the water

Moments from Prairie Creek Marina

Just across North Park Road from the Prairie Creek Marina and recreation area — one of the most established boating gateways on Beaver Lake's western shore, with fuel dock, slips and ramp.

Protected land

Near Hobbs State Park–Conservation Area, Arkansas's largest state park, with trails and shoreline held permanently undeveloped — a green edge that can't be built against.

Connected

A short drive to downtown Rogers and Bentonville — the Northwest Arkansas hub anchored by Walmart's global headquarters and the XNA regional airport.
