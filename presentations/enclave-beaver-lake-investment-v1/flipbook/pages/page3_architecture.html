The architecture

Modern homes that live with the lake.

Stone, timber and glass, drawn low into the wooded grade — living roofs, infinity edges, and walls that disappear toward the water. Each homesite is oriented for light and lake views, with courtyard pools and deep covered decks that carry the living space outside.
