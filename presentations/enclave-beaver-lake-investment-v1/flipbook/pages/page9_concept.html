The Enclave rises in the wooded hills above the Prairie Creek arm of Beaver Lake — a 28,000-acre Ozark reservoir of deep, clean water, with the marina and open lake just minutes from the gate.

Orientation
The parcel lies southwest of Prairie Creek Marina, set back from the shoreline.
Marina, fuel dock and lake access are minutes northeast.
Rogers, Bentonville and Hobbs State Park lie a short drive beyond.
