The honest conclusion: The as-is floor is high — the fixer comp (54 Elizabeth) sold at $205K, the current Homes.com average is $227K, and RPR's condition-adjusted value is $207K. At the current asking (~$210K), the all-in cost reaches ~$315K against a $272K resale — no flip margin. The deal only works as a flip if acquired below current comps, ideally ≤ ~$145K. If the realistic buy is ~$190K or higher, the stronger thesis is buy-improve-and-hold (rental), not a quick flip.

Comp-based broker opinion — not an appraisal or financial advice. Renovation figures are planning estimates that contractor bids could move materially. Summary: ARV ~$272K · rehab ~$75K · purchase target ~$130K–$145K.
